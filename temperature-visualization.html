Max Temperature vs. Latitude


The plot showing City Latitude vs. Max Temperature (01/05/17) shows that there is a significant correlation between latitude and max temperature; the farther north you go away from the equator is correlated to colder temperatures. Since it was winter in the northern hemisphere and summer in the southern hemisphere on the date of recording max temperatures, the scatter plot fits an expected pattern.
