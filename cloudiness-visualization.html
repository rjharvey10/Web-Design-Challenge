Cloudiness vs. Latitude


The plot showing City Latitude vs. Cloudiness (01/05/17) shows that there isn't a significant correlation between latitude and cloudiness. Based on the scatter plot of the data, it appears that cloudiness percentage can only be measured in increments of 5, which skews the data. The results confirm what is expected, which is that cloudiness is not correlated to, or determined by, latitude.
